## Design Decisions

### Why Vanilla JavaScript?

Provides excellent performance without requiring additional frameworks.

### Why Responsive Design?

Ensures an optimal user experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

### Why Modular Sections?

Allows recruiters to quickly navigate through skills, experience, education, and projects.

### Why Minimal UI?

Maintains a clean and professional appearance while emphasizing content over excessive visual effects.
